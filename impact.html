Grace: Production Lead

Women's Empowerment Spotlight

Before: Inconsistent farm labor income After: Steady income as Production Lead Impact: Children's school fees secured
"Because of this project, I can now earn a steady income and support my children's school fees. I feel proud of the skills I've gained and the role I play in building our community."

Before joining: Grace relied on inconsistent farm labor, struggling to make ends meet and unable to consistently support her children's education.

Today: Grace supervises brick pressing operations, teaches new workers essential skills, and has opened a small savings account for her family. She earns a reliable income that allows her to plan for her children's future.

David: Youth Trainee

Youth Employment Spotlight

Before: Limited job opportunities After: Skilled operator & mentor Impact: Saving for technical training
"I learned how to operate the brick press and now I earn enough to help my parents and save for my technical training."

Before the initiative: David faced limited job opportunities in the village, with few prospects for meaningful employment or skills development.

After training: David now operates the brick press with confidence, earns reliable income, and mentors younger trainees. He's saving money to pursue technical training that will further advance his career.

The Wamalwa Family

Affordable Housing Story

Cost Savings: 35% less Timeline: Completed before rainy season Quality: Strong and durable bricks
"These bricks helped us finish our home in time for the rains. We could afford them, and they are strong and durable."

Impact: The Wamalwa family saved nearly 35% in construction costs compared to conventional materials, enabling them to complete their home before the rainy season. The quality and affordability of Ndakaru bricks made homeownership achievable for this family.

Environmental Outcomes

  • Improved kiln efficiency: We are working toward cleaner firing methods and minimizing wood consumption per batch, reducing emissions compared to unmanaged traditional kilns.
  • Locally sourced materials: Using locally available soil reduces transportation emissions and construction costs.
  • Sustainable land-use: Our practices ensure minimal land dereliction and ecological disturbance, protecting the environment for future generations.
