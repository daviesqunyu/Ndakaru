From Plot to Keys: A Family’s Journey

February 2026 · 6 min read

When the Kamau family bought their plot in Sirisia, Bungoma’s they had a clear dream: a three-bedroom bungalow with a small garden. They also had a tight budget and had heard horror stories about contractors disappearing mid-project.

After comparing three firms, they chose Ndakaru Brick and Construction. We started with a site visit, then a detailed BOQ and a phased payment plan tied to milestones. Foundation, slab, walls, roof, and finishing each had a clear deadline and sign-off.

“We got our keys on the date we were promised. No drama, no hidden costs. That’s rare in this industry.” — Mr. Kamau

Communication was mainly through WhatsApp and weekly site meetings. The family visited every Saturday to see progress. Eight months later, they moved in. Today they’re planning a perimeter wall and gate with us for phase two.
