About Our Initiative

The Ndakaru Community Brick Manufacturing initiative produces environmentally friendly building bricks using locally available soil and manual brick presses. We are committed to providing dignified employment, skills development, and income opportunities for our community members who previously had limited economic prospects.

Current Challenge

The project is facing financial constraints that threaten operational continuity. Limited capital has affected our ability to purchase key inputs and maintain equipment. If production slows down, 25 families may lose their primary source of livelihood.

How You Can Help

We seek financial assistance or partnership support to:

  • Purchase improved brick molds and a manual or semi-automated press
  • Construct a sheltered brick drying shed for all-weather production
  • Provide tools, gloves, boots, and basic safety equipment for workers
